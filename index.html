About Us

Monument Health Family Health Education Services, located in Spearfish, South Dakota, provides a full range of reproductive health services tailored to support individuals and families in our community. Our income-based services include access to birth control options, STI screenings, pregnancy testing, and comprehensive annual exams. We are dedicated to offering compassionate, affordable care and empowering our patients with health education that meets their unique needs.
